Frequently Asked Questions

The main, plan, history, and providers pages all contain a search bar, in which a search query can be made.

For all search queries, you must select whether to search for a movie or TV show by selecting the desired option within the relevant dropdown.

A basic search can be made by just having the production type (movie or TV show) selected and simply clicking the ‘Search’ button. You will then be presented with the results of currently trending movies or TV shows.

Selecting the type of genre and the production type within the relevant dropdowns and then clicking ‘Search’ will return results of currently trending movies or TV shows of the selected genre.

By typing into the search box, having a production type selected, and then clicking ‘Search’, you can perform a more refined search that presents you with results according to what has been entered in the search box.

For an even more refined search, having the type of genre and the production type selected, along with text entered in the search box will present results according to what has been entered in the search box, as well as matching the type of genre and production type selected.
By default, when performing a search with the search box empty, the most trending results are presented. When performing a search with the search box populated, the results that best match the search query are presented.

The results can be sorted by popularity by simply selecting ‘Popularity’ within the ‘SortBy’ drop down located near the top right corner of the results page, and then clicking on ‘Apply’. This will refresh the results so that they are sorted accordingly.
When signed in, clicking ‘MyProviders’ within the ‘More’ dropdown located on the right of the top banner will take you to the provider selection page.

On this page, a collection of supported streaming providers can be viewed by clicking on the buttons to either see providers for movies or providers for TV shows. Each provider listed has an ‘Add’ button next to them, which can be clicked on in order to have them selected.

With the desired streaming providers selected, a search query can then be made using the search bar by selecting the type of production (movie or TV show) and optionally, the type of genre as well. The presented results will match the providers that have been selected as long as a search is made with the search box being empty.
By simply clicking on the poster picture belonging to a movie or TV show, you can see its details.

These poster pictures are used to represent a movie or TV show on many pages throughout the application, such as the main page, results pages, the MyPlan page, and the Watch History page.
A movie or TV show’s streaming service availability can be viewed on its details page, which can be accessed by clicking on its associated poster picture, used to represent a movie or TV show all throughout the application, such as on the main page, results pages, the MyPlan page, and the Watch History page.

Streaming service availability can also be seen when clicking on the ‘Add to My Plan’ button underneath a poster picture on the main page or results pages.
Many features of this application such as the plan and watch history are unique to each user, and must be tied to an account. In order to be able to access these features, you must first have an account, and then ensure that you are logged in. You will then be able to access the desired pages under the ‘More’ dropdown located on the right of the top banner.
Simply clicking on the ‘Movie Night Planner’ logo located in the middle of the top banner from any page will direct you to the main page.
To be able to access your plan, you must first be logged in to the application. Once logged in, clicking on ‘MyPlan’ within the ‘More’ dropdown located to the right of the top banner will direct you to the MyPlan page.

The MyPlan page allows you to view all movies and TV shows you have added to your plan. Clicking on the calendar icon on the date selector at the top of the page, selecting a date, and then clicking ‘Select’ will allow you to see what you have added on a specific day.
There are multiple ways in which a movie or TV show can be added to your plan.

One way is by clicking on the ‘Add to My Plan’ button underneath each poster picture shown on the main page, as well as the results pages when entering a search query. A window will appear in which you can select the date on which you plan to watch the chose movie or TV show. Clicking on the calendar icon, selecting a date on the calendar, and then clicking on ‘Select’ will then add the movie or TV show to your plan on the chosen date.

A movie or TV show can also be added to your plan through its associated details page. By clicking on the ‘Add to My Plan’ button at the top of the page, a similar window will appear in which you can select the date for the chosen movie or TV show to be added to your plan. Simply click on the calendar icon, select the date, and then click ‘Select’ to add to your plan.
Movies or TV shows that aren’t currently available on any streaming services do not have the option to be added to your plan.
Entries in your plan can be deleted by clicking on the ‘Edit Plan’ button located below the date selector at the top of the page. Once clicked, ‘Delete’ buttons should appear under each entry. When clicking on the ‘Delete’ button under the desired entry, a confirmation window will appear, in which clicking on ‘Confirm’ will delete the entry.
Clicking on the ‘Edit Plan’ button located below the date selector at the top of the page will allow for ‘Change Date’ buttons to appear under each entry. When clicking on the ‘Change Date’ button under the desired entry, a window will appear in which the new date can be specified by clicking on the calendar button and then selecting the desired date. Once a new date has been selected, it can be confirmed by clicking the ‘Confirm’ button.
To view your watch history, you must first be logged in. Once logged in, it can be accessed by clicking on ‘History’ within the ‘More’ dropdown located the right of the top banner.

The Watch History page allows you to view movies and TV shows that you have marked as watched. Entering text into the text field at the top of the page, and then clicking ‘Search’ will return results of any matching movie or TV show that you have in your watch history.
When on the details page of a movie or TV show, which can be accessed by clicking on an associated poster picture found all throughout the application, clicking on the ‘Already Watched?’ button located at the top of the page will open a window in which you can select the date on which you watched the movie or TV show. Simply click on the calendar icon, select the date from the calendar, and click ‘Select’ to add to your watch history.
Each entry on the Watch History page has a set of buttons beside it, with one being a ‘Delete’ button. When clicking on the ‘Delete’ button next to a desired entry, a confirmation popup will appear asking you to confirm the deletion. Clicking ‘OK’ will delete the entry.
Each entry on the Watch History page has an ‘Edit’ button beside it. When clicking this button, a window will appear in which you are able to select a new date, this can be done by clicking the calendar icon and selecting a date on the calendar. To confirm the new date, simply click ‘Select’.
When clicking on ‘Login’ within the ‘More’ dropdown located to the right of the top banner, you will be taken to the Login page. Underneath the text fields for username and password, there is a ‘Registration’ button, clicking on this will take you to the Registration page in which you can enter your details to create an account.
Clicking on ‘Login’ within the ‘More’ dropdown located to the right of the top banner will take you to the Login page in which you can enter your username and password in the relevant text fields and then press ‘Login’ to attempt to log in.

If successful, you will be able to have full access to all the features offered by the application.
By clicking on the ‘Dark Mode’ toggle within the ‘More’ dropdown located to the right of the top banner, you can either enable or disable it.
